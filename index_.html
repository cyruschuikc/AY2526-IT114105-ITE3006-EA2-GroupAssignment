About Us

The KKCB Camping Club, also known as KamiKiCyBe Camping Club, was founded to bring together outdoor enthusiasts across Hong Kong. Our mission is to create a welcoming community where people of all ages can explore nature, share camping knowledge, and build lasting friendships.

We organize regular events including camping trips, hiking excursions, eco‑friendly workshops, and family gatherings. Each activity is designed to foster a spirit of adventure while promoting sustainability and respect for the environment.

The club is maintained by dedicated volunteers and collaborators, including Cyrus CHUI KA CHUN and Tang Hoi Fung, who ensure our website and community remain vibrant and accessible.

(Click here to learn more)

Since its founding, KKCB Camping Club has grown into a hub for individuals who value outdoor living and cultural exchange. We believe camping is more than just an activity — it is a lifestyle that blends adventure, creativity, and community spirit. Whether you are a seasoned camper or a beginner, you’ll find opportunities to connect, learn, and enjoy nature with us.
