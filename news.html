April 5, 2026 — Gear Swap Meet

Members Gather for Sustainable Gear Exchange

Hong Kong Camping Club hosted its first ever gear swap meet, drawing dozens of members eager to exchange tents, lanterns, and cooking sets. The event was not only about trading equipment but also about promoting sustainability. “It’s great to see old gear find new life,” said longtime member Lee Ming. The meet sparked lively discussions on eco-friendly camping practices and reducing waste, reinforcing the club’s commitment to responsible outdoor living.

April 10, 2026 — Spring Camping Event Announced

Annual Spring Adventure Returns to Sai Kung

Hong Kong Camping Club is excited to announce its annual Spring Camping Event at Sai Kung. Scheduled for two days, the event will feature guided hikes, campfire cooking, and team-building games. Families and solo adventurers alike are welcome. Registration opened today, and organizers expect spots to fill quickly. “This is our flagship event — a chance to reconnect with nature and each other,” said organizer Tang Hoi Fung.

April 15, 2026 — Photography Contest Launch

Capturing the Spirit of Camping

The club launched its first photography contest, inviting members to showcase the beauty of Hong Kong’s landscapes and camping life. Categories include “Best Landscape,” “Community Spirit,” and “Wildlife Moments.” Winning entries will be featured on the club’s website and social media. Prizes include camping gear vouchers. “We want to celebrate the artistry of our members,” said branding lead Cyrus CHUI KA CHUN. Submissions close May 10.

April 18, 2026 — Trail Cleanup Day

Volunteers Restore Tai Tam Country Park

Today, dozens of members joined forces to clear litter and restore hiking paths at Tai Tam Country Park. The cleanup improved the environment and strengthened community spirit. Volunteers shared stories, enjoyed refreshments, and pledged to continue protecting Hong Kong’s natural treasures. “It’s about giving back to the trails we love,” said participant Mei Wong. The initiative will become a monthly tradition.

March 28, 2026 — New Hiking Trail Partnership

Club Partners with Local Conservation Groups

Hong Kong Camping Club has partnered with local conservation groups to maintain and explore new hiking trails. This collaboration ensures safe, accessible paths while promoting environmental stewardship. Members will be invited to join monthly trail walks and maintenance sessions, reinforcing the club’s role in preserving Hong Kong’s natural heritage.

February 20, 2026 — Community BBQ Night

Food, Music, and Friendship at Clearwater Bay

The club’s BBQ night was a feast of flavors and friendship. Members grilled seafood, shared homemade dishes, and enjoyed live music. The event strengthened bonds within the community and reminded everyone that camping culture thrives on togetherness. “It felt like family,” said attendee Jason Lau.

January 25, 2026 — Campfire Storytelling Night

Stories and Folklore Warm Winter Night

On a chilly evening at Tai Mo Shan, members gathered around the fire to share personal camping tales and folklore. The warmth of the flames and richness of the stories created an unforgettable atmosphere. This tradition will continue as a seasonal highlight, blending culture and community in the outdoors.
