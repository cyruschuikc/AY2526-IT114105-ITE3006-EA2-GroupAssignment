Our Mission

The Hong Kong Camping Club was founded to unite outdoor enthusiasts across Hong Kong. Our mission is to create a welcoming community where people of all ages can explore nature, share camping knowledge, and build lasting friendships.

What We Do

  • Camping Trips: Weekend getaways to Hong Kong’s scenic countryside.
  • Hiking Excursions: Guided hikes across trails from beginner to advanced levels.
  • Eco-Friendly Workshops: Hands-on sessions promoting sustainability and environmental awareness.
  • Family Gatherings: Inclusive activities designed for all ages to enjoy the outdoors together.

Our Community
