1. Pui O Campsite

Pui O Campsite is one of the most popular and well‑equipped campsites on Lantau Island, situated right beside the scenic Pui O Beach. It offers a total of 54 camping bays, all free of charge, with each bay fitted with basic facilities such as tables, benches and barbecue pits.

Shared facilities include clean toilets, shower areas, and water supply points, making it convenient for campers to stay overnight comfortably. The campsite is family‑friendly, easily accessible by public transport, and surrounded by a peaceful natural environment with sea views nearby.

It operates on a first‑come, first‑served basis and is closed every Tuesday for regular cleaning and maintenance.

2. Ngong Ping Campsite

Ngong Ping Campsite is a quiet and scenic campsite located on a highland plateau in Ma On Shan Country Park. It features open grassland with panoramic views of the Sai Kung seas, distant islands and rolling hills, making it ideal for campers who enjoy nature and quietness.

The site provides around 20 camping spots, each equipped with barbeque pits, tables and benches. Basic pit toilets are available on site, but campers will need to bring their own drinking water.

It is less crowded than urban campsites and is popular among hiking and camping enthusiasts. The campsite can be reached on foot in about 40 minutes from the Ma On Shan Barbecue Area.

3. Sai Wan Campsite

Sai Wan Campsite is a stunning beachfront campsite located beside Sai Wan Beach in Sai Kung East Country Park, along Section 2 of the MacLehose Trail. As one of the famous Four Bays of Tai Long Wan, it offers breathtaking views of clear blue waters, offshore islands and impressive sunrise scenes over the ocean.

The site is especially popular among hikers and campers who enjoy coastal wilderness scenery. Facilities include standard toilets and tap water supply, though there are no dedicated barbecue pits or shower facilities. Campers should also take care regarding high tide levels, as the site lies directly on the beachfront.

4. Tai Mong Tsai Campsite

Tai Mong Tsai Campsite is a compact and well-facilitated campsite located in Sai Kung West Country Park. It is known for its excellent accessibility, making it an ideal choice for camping beginners and families seeking a convenient outdoor experience.

Surrounded by trees, the campsite provides a calm and shaded atmosphere right next to the main road, allowing easy access by public transport or private car. Facilities include pavilions, barbecue pits, tables, benches, clotheslines and tap water, while flushing toilets are available a short walk away. The area also offers pleasant walking routes with views of Sai Kung’s islands and nearby country park scenery.

5. Nam Shan Campsite

Nam Shan Campsite is a spacious and well-developed campsite located in Lantau South Country Park along South Lantau Road. Surrounded by quiet woodland and green valleys, it provides a sheltered, relaxing environment perfect for family and group camping.

It features extensive facilities including pavilions, barbecue pits, tables and benches, camping platforms, cooking stoves, flushing toilets and cold showers. It is also home to one of the largest barbecue areas in Lantau’s country parks. A short walk leads to a viewpoint overlooking Mui Wo and Pui O Bay, and the campsite is conveniently close to Lantau Trail sections and local beach areas.
