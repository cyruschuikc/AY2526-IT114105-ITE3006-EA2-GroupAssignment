Welcome to Our Site

Hi, Visitor! Welcome to our club website — Hong Kong Camping Club.
We are the premier free-lifestyle outdoor adventures group, hosting camping events
and community activities for nature lovers in Hong Kong.

Our mission is to connect people who love the outdoors, share camping knowledge, and build a vibrant community.
Join us for events, explore new sites, and experience nature together!

Why join HK Camping Club

  • Join a vibrant outdoor community

    Meet fellow campers, hikers, and nature lovers who share your passion for the outdoors.

  • Access exclusive events and activities

    Take part in camping trips, hiking excursions, BBQ nights, and eco-friendly workshops all year round.

  • Learn and share practical outdoor skills

    Get tips on camping gear, camp cooking, route planning, and Leave No Trace principles from experienced members.

  • Explore Hong Kong’s best outdoor spots

    Discover hidden gems and popular campsites with our curated routes and insider recommendations.

  • Promote sustainable and responsible camping

    Help us protect the environment by learning and practicing eco-friendly camping habits together.
